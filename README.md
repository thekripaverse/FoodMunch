## Project Overview

FoodMuch is a user-friendly food delivery website designed to provide a seamless online food ordering experience. The platform enables users to explore restaurants, browse menus, and discover a variety of cuisines and offers from a single interface. The goal of this project is to demonstrate a clean, responsive, and intuitive front-end design for a real-world food delivery platform.

FoodMuch focuses on usability, visual clarity, and structured presentation of restaurant and menu information to enhance the overall customer experience.

---

## Key Features

* **User-Friendly Interface**
  Simple and intuitive design for smooth navigation and easy ordering experience.

* **Restaurant Listings**
  Displays a variety of restaurants offering multiple cuisines.

* **Search and Filter Options**
  Allows users to quickly find restaurants or specific dishes.

* **Menu Details**
  Shows structured menus with images, pricing, and available offers.

* **Responsive Design**
  Optimized layout for different screen sizes.
